# Helixar.site

**Create content at the speed you think.**

Helixar is an AI-powered video analysis platform built specifically for content creators.

Upload your own videos (or trending competitor videos) and let the AI deeply analyze them. It learns **your unique creative style** — hook timing, scene pacing, retention factors, and unconscious patterns — then generates authentic scripts, ideas, hooks, and platform-ready content that actually sounds like **you**, not generic AI.

### ✨ Key Features

- Decode why your best videos worked (hooks, pacing, retention)
- Analyze competitor videos and adapt winning strategies to your style
- Generate authentic scripts 10x faster
- Test multiple hooks with predicted performance
- Spot your personal creative patterns and consistency
- Turn 1 long video into 10+ platform versions (YouTube → TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Conversational chat interface for brainstorming and refining
- Live activity feed — Helixar works while you sleep
- Continuous learning: the more you upload, the better it knows you

### 🚀 Benefits

- **10,000+** videos analyzed
- Saves creators **5.2 hours per week** on average
- Double your content output without losing your voice
- Go from stuck to posting consistently

### Integrations

YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, CapCut, Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, Notion, Google Drive, Dropbox, Frame.io, Vimeo, and more.

### How to Use

1. Go to **[helixar.site](https://helixar.site)**
